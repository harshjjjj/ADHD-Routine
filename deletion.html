Data Deletion Request

At Truce, we believe your data belongs to you. Because Truce stores your routines and task completions locally on your device, our servers do not have access to your personal routine data.

For Guest Users

If you use Truce without creating an account, simply uninstalling the app from your device will permanently delete all your data.

For Signed-in Users

If you created an account to back up your data, you can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly within the app:

  1. Open the Truce app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion

Manual Request

If you no longer have access to the app or your device, but want to ensure your email address is removed from our authentication servers, please email our support team from the email address associated with your account.
