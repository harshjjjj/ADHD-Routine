Privacy Policy

How we protect your mind, your workflow, and your data.

💡 ADHD-First Principle: Your routines, checklists, and habits are stored entirely on your own local device. Truce does not monitor your tasks, sell your routines, or use creepy ad-tracking. Your routines belong to you alone.

1. Introduction and Scope

Welcome to Truce ("Application", "we", "us", "our"), a product designed to support neurodiverse, ADHD, and executive-dysfunction-prone users in building morning checklist workflows. Consistent with Apple's App Store Review guidelines and Google Play developer requirements, this Privacy Policy outlines our transparent data handling guidelines.

This Privacy Policy applies to the Truce website (truceapp.site), our mobile applications, waitlist forms, support services, and active client environments.

2. Data We Collect and Where It Lives

To preserve complete academic and visual autonomy, we split data collection into two separate categories:

A. Local-Only On-Device Action Data (Routine & Tracker)

Any checklists, routine setups, milestone achievements, custom times, or Rest Mode configurations you create inside the mobile app or browser engine are stored 100% locally on your own sandboxed device storage (e.g. standard browser localStorage, IndexedDB, or local cellular caches).

  • We do not receive, access, or log the lists, tasks, or names of your routines.
  • We do not record active habit streaks, dopamine scores, or rest times on outer server environments.
  • Your data is safe from breaches because it never leaves your physical cell phone or iPad.

B. Waitlist and Notification Signups (Server-Side)

When you voluntarily join the early-access waitlist, submit feedback, or request features through our website, we securely transmit following records to our server database:

  • Email Address: Used strictly to notify you of beta spots and app release details.
  • User Feedback: Any voluntary features, preferences, likes, dislikes, or recommendations you provide inside our waitlist forms.
  • Log Metadata: The creation timestamp (e.g. "Just Now") and queue positioning.

3. Third-Party Services and Subprocessors

We restrict third-party SDKs to prevent the sensory noise, screen flickering, and privacy invasion common in generic habit trackers. We use only the following reputable, secure processors:

  • Google Firebase (Firestore & Hosting): Secure, sandboxed cloud infrastructure to host the landing page and database waitlist records.
  • EmailJS Applet Sync: If the user is registered for automatic reply emails, we safely relay their email coordinates using EmailJS API networks to dispatch a single, voluntary welcome thank-you note. No advertising tracking is attached.
  • Google Sheets API (Admin-Only): Relayed coordinates are synced strictly to admin-operated sheets for beta priority sorting. No marketing syndicates have access.

4. Cookies & Diagnostic Analytics

Our site does not serve marketing cookies. We do not integrate Facebook Pixel, AdWords tags, or any other retargeting networks. We may use highly minimalist, privacy-first local diagnostics (like noting screen widths and height metrics) to ensure our responsive grids render beautifully on mobile devices.

5. How to Easily Delete Your Data

In absolute compliance with Apple End-User Data guidelines and Google Play User Data policies, we enable immediate data deletion:

  • Local Data: To immediately clear your on-device routine configuration, simply clear your browser's history/local storage or tap "Reset App and Cache" inside our application dashboard.
  • Waitlist & Feedback Data: If you wish to permanently erase your waitlist email and text notes from our primary database files, please access our dedicated Data Deletion Request Portal. Submissions are processed automatically within 24 hours.

6. Medical Disclaimer & Self-Care Boundaries

In strict compliance with Apple App Store Guideline 1.4.1 (Physical Safety and Medical Apps) and the Google Play Health Content Policy, we declare the following operational boundaries:

⚠️ TRUCE IS NOT A MEDICAL APP or MEDICAL CLINICAL ENTITY. All interactive checklists, focus meters, energy tracking metrics, and recommendations served by this app are engineered strictly as personal productivity, time-blocking, and gentle cognitive behavioral organization aids. No software can or should replace professional psychiatric help, psychodynamic therapy, ADHD diagnosis, or medication prescription schedules. Please consult with your licensed behavioral physician, clinical health psychologist, or psychiatrist before instituting any dramatic lifestyle, behavioral, or psychiatric treatment alterations.

7. GDPR & CCPA Consumer Rights

Even though we operate as an independent, indie developer studio, we respect international privacy standards. Users located in the European Union (under GDPR) or California (under CCPA) possess complete rights to:

  1. Access any waitlist records we hold.
  2. Request rectification of incorrect details.
  3. Object to receiving notifications.
  4. Execute a full "Right to Be Forgotten" deletion (available via deletion.html).

For inquiries regarding these parameters, please contact our support desk directly at hello@truceapp.site.

8. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Truce is built to support individuals of all age ranges, including students dealing with executive dysfunction. We do not knowingly collect personal or demographic records from children under the age of 13. If you believe your child has submitted an email on our waitlist, contact us at hello@truceapp.site and we will immediately execute permanent database sanitization.

9. Security Safeguards

We employ Secure Socket Layer (SSL/HTTPS) encryption across all communication networks. Database entries are guarded by secure Firestore protection policies in compliance with our cloud infrastructure guidelines.
