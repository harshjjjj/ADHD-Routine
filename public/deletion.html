Our Deletion Protocol

In accordance with Apple App Store Developer Guideline 5.1.1(v) (Account Deletion) and Google Play Safety regulations, Truce provides complete, immediate power to erase your records.

1. Clearing Local Device Assets (Immediate)

Since 100% of your checklist entries, active streaks, energy settings, and notes live solely in local storage variables on your physical device, we do not have a server copy to erase!

👉 To immediately wipe everything locally, tap the "Reset Cache" link in your dashboard, or clear your mobile browser's cookies and site cache.

2. Erasing Waitlist & Support History

If you signed up to join our waitlist, left feedback, or contacted support, those coordinates are saved in our secure Google Cloud Firestore.

  • What is deleted: Your email, queue rank, registration dates, diagnostic feedback text, and helpdesk records.
  • Duration: Submitting the right-hand form queues your data for permanent deletion within 24 hours.
