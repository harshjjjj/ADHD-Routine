Frequently Asked Questions

💡 What makes Truce different from regular habit apps?
Traditional trackers punish you with broken streaks, red red marks, and irritating alert badges when you miss a day. Truce is built with zero punishment mechanisms or "shame cycles." It adapts to your energy level and allows you to pause or skip guilt-free.
🔒 Is my daily routine private and secure?
Absolutely. Every checklist, schedule, task, and performance note you author inside Truce is stored locally in sandboxed variables on your private device. No one else has access to your daily actions.
📱 Does Truce support custom notifications?
Yes, you can establish calming and custom localized reminders tailored specifically to your visual pace without passive-aggressive push notifications.
🗑️ How do I opt-out or erase my waitlist record?
We support immediate data sanitization. If you wish to permanently erase your waitlist email or support requests from our cloud nodes, navigate directly to our Data Deletion page.
