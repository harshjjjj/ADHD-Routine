🛡️ The Truce Pact & Policy

At Truce, we take a radical ADHD-first approach to daily life. This document serves as our policy, philosophy framework, and explicit agreement.

Mitigating the "Hidden Risk" of Truce

The only risk with the name Truce is that to a highly cynical user, a "truce" might imply a weak compromise — a state where neither side gets what they want, leading to a feeling of stagnation.

We explicitly reject this cynical definition.

What the Truce Actionably Is:
The Truce is an active, compassionate agreement between your current energy level and your daily goals.

Instead of forced compliance or rigid tracking, Truce operates under the law of dynamic capacity. On a high-energy day, your goals stay elevated. On a low-energy day, your routine dynamically soft-scales to support you. Neither side "loses" — your brain wins by maintaining continuity, and your body wins by avoiding burnout.

Our Data Privacy Promise

Your daily checklists, routines, schedules, and reflection notes are stored entirely locally on your device. We do not track, process, sell, or monitor your personal routines. Your ADHD journey is yours alone to see and navigate.

Terms of Gentle Service

You are hereby permitted to skip days, toggle Rest Mode, and adjust goals without penalty, guilt letters, or push alert passive-aggression.
