Common Questions

Designed for different brains. Here is how Truce works, why it is different, and how it helps you thrive without the guilt.

How is Truce different from standard routine trackers?
+

Traditional routine trackers are built for neurotypical minds. They demand unbroken streaks and make you feel guilty when life gets in the way. Truce is built explicitly for ADHD and neurodiverse coping mechanisms. Instead of streaks, we measure long-term completion percentages. If you miss a day, you don't lose your momentum, making it much easier to pick up where you left off.

What is a "guilt-free routine builder"?
+

Your focus and energy levels change daily. On some days, you have high focus; on others, getting out of bed is a victory. Truce allows you to adjust your routine's difficulty on the fly based on your current dopamine energy level (Low, Standard, or High). Checking off a simplified 2-minute version of your routine on a low-energy day still counts as 100% success!

How does the weekly overview help with object permanence?
+

For many of us, out of sight means out of mind. Truce uses persistent visual widget cards, color-coded energy gauges, and a playful "smiley-face calendar" instead of intimidating checklists. It offers immediate sensory rewards and gentle visual cues to keep your routines visible and engaging, without resorting to nagging pings.

Will Truce always remain free?
+

The core guilt-free tracker, flexible energy adjuster, and milestone boards will always remain free. By joining the early-access waitlist today, you are guaranteed a lifetime free beta spot. As we grow, we will introduce completely optional premium modules (like deeper team synchronization, advanced analytical insights, and integrated custom soundscapes) that help us fund independent development.

How and when will I get my early access invitation?
+

We are releasing early access spots in small batches to preserve community quality and let us implement feedback incrementally. When your spot is ready, you will receive an automatic invite with download instructions. Make sure to whitelist hello@truceapp.site in your mail client so you don't miss your entry ticket!

🛡️ The Truce Pact & Policy

At Truce, we take a radical ADHD-first approach to daily life. This document serves as our policy, philosophy framework, and explicit agreement.

Mitigating the "Hidden Risk" of Truce

The only risk with the name Truce is that to a highly cynical user, a "truce" might imply a weak compromise — a state where neither side gets what they want, leading to a feeling of stagnation.

We explicitly reject this cynical definition.

What the Truce Actionably Is:
The Truce is an active, compassionate agreement between your current energy level and your daily goals.

Instead of forced compliance or rigid tracking, Truce operates under the law of dynamic capacity. On a high-energy day, your goals stay elevated. On a low-energy day, your routine dynamically soft-scales to support you. Neither side "loses" — your brain wins by maintaining continuity, and your body wins by avoiding burnout.

Our Data Privacy Promise

Your daily checklists, routines, schedules, and reflection notes are stored entirely locally on your device. We do not track, process, sell, or monitor your personal routines. Your ADHD journey is yours alone to see and navigate.

Terms of Gentle Service

You are hereby permitted to skip days, toggle Rest Mode, and adjust goals without penalty, guilt letters, or push alert passive-aggression.
